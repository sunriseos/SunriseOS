Multiboot Information

Gives access to the multiboot information structure created by our bootloader (GRUB).

Bootloader (GRUB) passed its address in $ebx when we started.

Our bootstrap had the job of copy-ing it to a PAGE_SIZE aligned address, map it, and passed it to us in $ebx.

When kernel initializes we store this address in BOOT_INFO, and we can then access it at any moment by calling get_boot_information.
